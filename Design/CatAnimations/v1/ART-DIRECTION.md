# DockCat Orange v1 Art Direction

DockCat Orange v1 is a friendly right-facing orange cat that evolves the placeholder identity into production-owned artwork. Frames use a 150 × 110 point logical canvas exported at 2x (300 × 220 pixels), transparent sRGB PNG, with feet/visual anchor `(75, 35)`, carry anchor `(117, 73)`, and handoff size `36 × 24`.

## Visual brief

- Warm orange body `#EB842A`, lighter paws/muzzle `#FAAA4E`, cream muzzle/belly `#FFD891`, restrained darker stripes `#BE5E21`, and dark outline `#462C22`.
- Rounded 2D shapes with a clear dark outline of roughly 2–4 logical points so the silhouette remains readable at 0.5 scale and not noisy at 2.0 scale.
- Soft, minimal shading; lighting is neutral so horizontal mirroring for left travel remains acceptable.
- Eyes stay simple: sleeping/settling uses closed arcs, wake/presentation uses open dark oval eyes. Expressions must not distract from notifications.
- Tail and ear motion is limited to subtle breathing, waiting, and gait accents inside the validated safe canvas.
- Paws reach around the separate mini-card anchor but no card, text, icon, badge, logo, or notification content is baked into textures.
- Canonical art faces right. Left travel mirrors the complete hierarchy. Up/down side-Dock presentation rotates the complete cat/card hierarchy rather than adding vertical frame variants.
- Animation timing favors readable key poses: loop clips ease across frame boundaries, transition clips end in stable hold poses for Reduced Motion, Disable Walking, and Pause Visual Animations.
